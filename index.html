OUR HERITAGE

Foundation and Purpose

Founded in 1993, the Mercado Artesanal de Quito was created to preserve Ecuador’s cultural heritage by providing a platform for local artisans to showcase their handmade crafts. It promotes traditional Ecuadorian craftsmanship and serves as a key space for artisans to connect with both locals and tourists.

Cultural Significance

The market is vital for preserving Ecuador’s diverse traditions. From Andean textiles to Amazonian crafts, each product tells the story of Ecuador’s rich cultural identity. The market ensures these traditions are kept alive for future generations.

A Hub for Tourism and Community

The Mercado Artesanal is a must-visit for both tourists and locals. Visitors can purchase unique handmade items while learning about Ecuador’s history and traditions, and the market serves as a cherished community space for cultural celebration.

Legacy and Future

The Mercado Artesanal de Quito continues to evolve, supporting artisans while preserving Ecuador’s craftsmanship. It remains an essential symbol of the city’s commitment to its cultural roots and a key player in Ecuador’s future.
